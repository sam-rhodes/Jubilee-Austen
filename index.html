Hi, I'm a web developer, visual designer, & coffee drinker.

A little bit about me.

My home base is in Tacoma, Washington, about 40 minutes south of Starbucks' hometown. My journey into tech started when I was quarantined during the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020. I made the jump to coding after I realized what I was doing for work became considered "non-essential" and I found myself furloughed and struggling to receive unemployment benefits. This was my blessing in disguise: I wanted to change career paths, and it was now or never!

I've always had a passion to create, whatever it may be, and that's what I'm able to do today: create through the expression of HTML & CSS. Once I created my first website, I became hooked; what else could I create? Who can I help? My ultimate goal was to have the freedom & ability to travel, collaborate, and design with others for work. I now serve coffee during the day, and code at night. When I'm not working on a project, you can find me either at the gym or playing video games.
